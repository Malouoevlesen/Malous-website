About Malou..

If you have already checked out my website, you probably know by now that I am trying to make it as a web developer. Since this is the "about me" page, I should probably tell you some stuff about me. You know, just in case.

Hi. I am Malou, a pretty normal person fighting for what I wanna do in life. I try to get the best out of my life, and never take myself too seriously.

Gaming, cooking, traveling and spending time with my family is pretty much my favourite things to do.

As a kid I got quite heavy into gaming, and it has been one of my passions since then. I have always found the IT industry facinating, and hoped that someday I could be a part of it.

However this is not where my journey starts.

In 2017 I gratuated from optometry school. In the 3,5 years it took to educate myself, I was working along in a optician shop.
I liked my job, but I always had a feeling that something wasn't quite right.

I then decided it was time to fulfill my dreams. I quit my job, and started working from home.
I took in all opportunities I could. Worked on Codeacademy, Udemy, went to different courses and got all the help I could get from my fiancé who is in the IT industry.

So here I am. Some people don't get to accomplish their dreams for various reasons, but here I am. Trying to fulfill mine.
